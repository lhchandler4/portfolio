About Me

I'm originally from Chattanooga, Tennessee. I have a rather diverse background when it comes to both education as well as employment. I have worked jobs from a cryptologic linguist and analyst to marketing for a blockchain platform. I have education varying from biochemistry to international relations and business. The common theme however that I noticed was the degree which technology permeates through everything that I have done. This really inspired me to want to become more involved with specifically software and how through development it is possible to streamline and make processes easier and more efficient for users. The applications are endless and I wanted to be a part of an industry that will never cease to offer challenges and new opportunities to learn. I believe my diverse background will bring a unique perspective to software development.
